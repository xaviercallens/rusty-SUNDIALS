The Stiffness Wall: Wide separation of fast Alfvén waves and slow tearing modes forces explicit schemes into $\Delta t < 10^{-9}$ s constraints, while implicit Jacobians become severely ill-conditioned.

The Control Latency Wall: Positive Lyapunov exponents characteristic of chaotic plasmas destroy gradient histories. Evaluating continuous PDEs within the strict $< 1$ ms real-time hardware actuation budget is computationally unfeasible.
